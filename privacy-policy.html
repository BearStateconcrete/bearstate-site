Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 18, 2026

Information We Collect

Bear State Concrete Construction Inc. ("Bear State Concrete," "we," "us," or "our") collects information you provide directly to us when you fill out our contact or quote request form, including:

  • Name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Project details and description
  • SMS consent status and timestamp

We may also collect basic technical data such as browser type and IP address when you visit our website, solely for security and analytics purposes.

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your quote request and schedule on-site consultations
  • Send SMS messages related to your project inquiry, quote follow-up, and post-job review requests (with your consent)
  • Communicate with you by phone or email about your project
  • Improve our website and services

We do not use your information for automated decision-making or profiling.

SMS Messaging Consent

By providing your phone number and checking the consent box on our website, you agree to receive SMS messages from Bear State Concrete LLC regarding your project inquiry, quote follow-up, and post-job review requests. Message frequency varies but will not exceed 4 messages per month. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP. We will never share your phone number or SMS opt-in consent with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.

SMS consent is optional. Declining to provide SMS consent will not affect your ability to receive a quote or use our services.

How We Share Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share your phone number or SMS opt-in consent with any third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes.

We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who assist in operating our business (such as CRM and messaging platforms), who are contractually bound to protect your data
  • When required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Bear State Concrete, our customers, or others

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP to any message we send

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the information below.
